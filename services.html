Our approach

At Knight Data & Automation, we don’t position ourselves as a subcontractor you only contact when something breaks.

We work alongside businesses as a trusted digital partner — helping them simplify technology, reduce friction, and build systems that support sustainable, long-term growth.

Everything we deliver is designed to be practical, understandable, and aligned with how your business actually operates day to day.

How we work

  • Understand first — we take time to understand your processes, challenges, and goals before recommending solutions.
  • Build on what you already have — wherever possible, we improve and extend existing tools rather than replacing them unnecessarily.
  • Keep it practical — systems are designed to be used daily, not admired once and forgotten.
  • Think long-term — decisions are made with scalability, sustainability, and future clarity in mind.

What we deliver

  • Managed IT & Microsoft 365

  • Automation & Power Apps
    Streamlining everyday processes with Power Automate and Power Apps — reducing manual effort and improving consistency across your business.
  • Reporting & Power BI
    Clear, meaningful reporting that turns data into insight — helping you understand performance and make confident decisions.
  • Technology consultancy
    Straightforward advice on systems, structure, and tooling — helping you invest wisely and avoid unnecessary complexity.

Our aim is simple: to make technology quieter, more useful, and better aligned with your business — now and in the future.
