Our services

Technology support, automation, and insight — delivered as a long-term digital partnership.

Our approach

At Knight Data & Automation, we don’t see ourselves as a subcontractor you only speak to when something breaks.

We work alongside businesses as a trusted digital partner — helping them use technology more effectively, reduce complexity, and build systems that support long-term growth.

Everything we deliver is designed to be practical, understandable, and aligned with how your business actually operates.

How we work

  • Understand first — we take time to understand your processes, challenges, and goals before recommending anything.
  • Build on what you already have — wherever possible, we improve and extend existing tools rather than replacing them.
  • Keep it practical — systems are designed to be used daily, not admired once and forgotten.
  • Think long-term — decisions are made with scalability, sustainability, and future clarity in mind.

What we deliver

  • Managed IT & Microsoft 365
    Proactive IT support, secure environments, and well-managed Microsoft 365 tenants — keeping your systems reliable, compliant, and easy to work with.
  • Automation & Power Apps
    Streamlining everyday processes with Power Automate and Power Apps — reducing manual work and improving consistency across your business.
  • Reporting & Power BI
    Clear, meaningful reporting that turns data into insight — helping you understand performance and make informed decisions.
  • Technology consultancy
    Practical advice on systems, structure, and tooling — helping you invest wisely and avoid unnecessary complexity.

Our aim is simple: to make technology quieter, more useful, and better aligned with your business — now and in the future.
